# Product Specification: KnitConnect

## Vision

KnitConnect is a marketplace connecting independent knitting instructors with learners, combining course sales, instructor-learner messaging, and a handmade-goods storefront in one platform.

## Target Audience

- Hobbyist and beginner knitters looking for structured, vetted instruction.
- Independent knitting instructors who currently rely on generic course platforms or social media to reach learners.

## Personas

### Learner — "Casual Casey"

- Wants to learn a specific technique (e.g., colorwork, cabling) from a real instructor, not a generic video library.
- Willing to pay for a course bundled with direct access to the instructor.

### Instructor — "Independent Ines"

- Currently splits sales across Etsy (products) and Skillshare/Udemy (courses), with no unified relationship to her audience.
- Needs a single place to sell courses, sell handmade goods, and message students.

## User Stories

- As a learner, I can browse and filter courses by technique and skill level.
- As a learner, I can message an instructor before or after purchasing a course.
- As a learner, I can buy handmade products from an instructor's storefront.
- As an instructor, I can create a course listing with pricing and curriculum.
- As an instructor, I can list handmade products for sale.
- As an instructor, I can respond to learner messages from one inbox.

## Product Requirements

- Course catalog with search and filtering.
- Checkout flow for courses and products (shared cart or separate flows — see Open Questions in the full spec process).
- Instructor profile pages with bio, course list, and storefront.
- In-app messaging between learner and instructor.
- Review system for courses and products.

## MVP Boundaries

In scope for MVP:

- Course catalog, course detail, and checkout.
- Instructor profile and storefront listing.
- Basic in-app messaging (text only).
- Reviews on completed courses/purchases.

Out of scope for MVP:

- Live video sessions.
- Recommendation engine.
- Multi-currency support.
- Mobile native apps.

## Assumptions

- Payments are handled via a third-party processor (not built in-house).
- Initial launch targets English-speaking markets only.
