# Payment Safety

## Consent

Payment requires explicit consent.

Consent should include:

- merchant
- item or service
- amount
- currency
- fees
- delivery or fulfillment terms
- payment method or account

## Limits

Define:

- per-transaction limit
- daily or monthly limit
- merchant allowlist or blocklist
- category restrictions
- approval threshold

## Verification

Before payment:

- verify merchant identity
- verify final price
- verify item details
- verify shipping or fulfillment
- verify refund/cancellation terms

## Rejection Cases

Reject or require approval when:

- amount changed
- merchant changed
- item substituted
- policy limit exceeded
- payment method unclear
- user intent ambiguous
